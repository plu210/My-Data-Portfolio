Technically, we, as the veteran, are not medically qualified to self diagnose. That said, it's nothing wrong to put down PTSD, depression or even write down mental health condition on the claim form. The VA will figure out what you have and change the name appropriately, same with tinnitus.

For your lay statement, just refer to your exam date and say (please see submitted treatment records page X) and mention when your symptoms started. There's nothing wrong with your first paragraph saying.

Statement in support of tinnitus

In year xxxx, I was at this base exposed to X. My symptoms appeared at X....

Then, at the bottom, after you've said your part.

5. Due to my MOS/AFSC XXX, I believe my tinnitus (or say diagnosed tinnitus if you can confirm that) is a direct result of my time in service.
